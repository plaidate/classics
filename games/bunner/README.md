# Bunner

An endless road, rail, and river crossing. *(Code the Classics Volume 1)*

![Bunner](screenshot.png)

## Controls

- D-pad — hop one cell in any direction
- Crank — each forward twist hops onward
- A — menus

## How it plays

Hop ever upward through grass, traffic, train tracks, and rivers.
Cars come in every speed; trains arrive seconds after the warning
bell; water is fatal unless you ride a log — which carries you with
it. The screen scrolls and the eagle takes anyone who lingers too
low. Score is the furthest row you reach.
